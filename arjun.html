Philanthropy & Social Impact

Inspired by my upbringing in Arghakhanchi, Nepal, I established a school fund worth 100,000 rupees at Shree Prabhakar Secondary School to support local students’ education and opportunities. Growing up in this community, I witnessed firsthand the challenges many children face in accessing quality learning resources. This fund aims to provide scholarships, learning materials, and essential support to empower students to pursue their academic dreams. Giving back to the place that shaped me has been both a personal mission and a way to create lasting impact for future generations in Arghakhanchi
